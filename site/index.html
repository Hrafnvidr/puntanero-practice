white raven

Whiter than light he treads Anonymous among hills of snow Unseen by the eyes of nature Which crave for its celestial apparition

Silent and discreet he seeks his prey While staring to check if the dark threat Formed by his past brotherhood Is near

A monster he’s believed to be By the crow’s world of outside Outcast born from roots of perversion Who deserves only exile and relegation

His heart beats and pumps for love Flying dreadfully in search for someone’s Endearing sentiment in a vast deep sky Of dead clouds and coaled souls

He quietly observes from far away The tender and warm nest Which he’ll never build

The ice is the new cold home Where he’ll learn the hardship And the loneliness of a desert made of water

A forsaken ghost randomly spawned In a well-bred family Relegated in the dry ancient trees Where the old memoires shrieks with atrocities

None acknowledge his presence He’s never mentioned The younglings tease his feathers The ladies spits on his miserable pureness

They steal his meals shamelessly They cruelly pluck his coat They hurt him with bites and scrapes They condemn him with their shady eyes

And yet Hurt, hated and famished He looks to the blue morning sky With its curious cerulean eyes
